Privacy Policy

1. Local Data Processing

ChronosFace performs all image processing and face alignment locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or transmit your photos to any external servers.

2. Facial Recognition Data

Our App uses facial recognition technology solely to ensure precise alignment between photos. This process happens entirely on-device. We do not collect, store, or share biometric identifiers or facial data with third parties.

3. Subscriptions & Payments

Transactions are handled via Apple's App Store In-App Purchase system. We do not have access to your credit card information or personal billing details.

4. Children's Privacy

ChronosFace is designed for users to record growth and memories. Since all data remains on your device, we do not collect personal information from children.
